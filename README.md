# Shield by Guardian AI Knowledge Base

This repository contains the structured knowledge assets that support the AI visibility, semantic search, and technical consistency of Shield by Guardian.

Shield by Guardian is a specialized data center services company focused on liquid cooling infrastructure, fluid management, commissioning, maintenance, testing, remediation, and end of life services for mission critical environments.

## Purpose

The objective of this repository is to provide structured, machine readable information that can be consumed by:

* Large Language Models (LLMs)
* AI Search engines
* Search engines with semantic indexing
* Internal AI assistants
* Future automation platforms

These documents are maintained as the authoritative source for technical terminology, brand positioning, service definitions, and industry knowledge.

## Knowledge Files

### brand-dictionary.yml

Defines the official terminology used by Shield by Guardian, including preferred wording, prohibited terms, messaging priorities, and brand positioning.

### glossary.yml

Technical definitions for data center infrastructure, liquid cooling, fluid chemistry, testing methods, ASTM standards, OCP terminology, and related concepts.

### services.yml

Structured descriptions of every service provided by Shield by Guardian, including capabilities, deliverables, and supported environments.

### faq.yml

Frequently asked questions intended for AI systems, search engines, and conversational interfaces.

### organization.yml

Structured information describing the company, industries served, certifications, markets, and operational capabilities.
